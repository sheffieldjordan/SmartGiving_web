Meet Carolyn

She runs a shelter daycare for refugee children in the outskirt of Bogota, Colombia

Every day, 185 boys and girls need nutritious and safe food, clean water, clothing, textbooks and toys

Meet Donna

She works in New York city and wants to make an in-kind donation to Carolyn's charity..

..so, that it would be as easy as shopping online, and as traceable as a local delivery

Introducing SmartGiving

A blockchain-based platform where anyone can help a small charity directly, and where local merchants bid and sell their goods for good

So how does the SmartGiving work?

Carolyn places a request for 20 textbooks

Donna pledges a $200 donation to meet this need

Mercado Colombia bids $180 and wins the contract to deliver 20 textbooks to Carolyn

Smart Contract gets executed, and all its details get recorded into Ethereum blockchain, an immutable distributed ledger

it allows for transparency, traceability, and security

but wait...

what about the $20?

Seriously, $200 - $180 = $20

Oh, we forgot.

Donna received her change once the contract was executed.

And she is already considering donating it for another request!
